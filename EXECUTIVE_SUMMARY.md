# Executive Summary - Laptop Market Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview

Comprehensive competitive analysis of the laptop market using multi-source data (Amazon, Flipkart, Reddit, YouTube) with AI-powered sentiment analysis, keyword extraction, and strategic recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Key Findings

### Market Leaders
1. **ASUS** - Premium leader (₹73,250 avg, 4.43 rating)
2. **Dell** - Quality leader (₹72,500 avg, 4.40 rating)
3. **Acer** - Value leader (₹55,000 avg, best price-to-quality)
4. **Lenovo** - Balanced player (₹61,750 avg, 4.38 rating)

### Customer Sentiment
- **Overall**: 65.2% Positive, 10.1% Negative, 24.6% Neutral
- **Best Source**: Reddit (80% positive sentiment)
- **Top Praised Features**: Price, Screen, RAM, Battery, Performance
- **Top Complaints**: Fan noise, RAM issues, Work performance

### Market Segmentation
- **Premium** (>₹70K): 25% - ASUS, Dell dominate
- **Mid-Range** (₹50-70K): 55% - Most competitive segment
- **Budget** (<₹50K): 20% - Acer leads
